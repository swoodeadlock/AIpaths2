AI Literacy Levels

Image source: Framework for AI Literacy | EDUCAUSE Review (published on June 3, 2024)

Level 1: Understand AI – Basic understanding of AI, such as machine learning, neural networks, and their applications in TCM.

Level 2: Use and Apply AI – Practical use of AI tools like diagnostic support systems and understanding how to use these tools effectively.

Level 3: Analyze and Evaluate AI – Critically reflect on AI tools, including their benefits, limitations, and ethical considerations.

Level 4: Create AI – Develop new AI-driven solutions for TCM, engage in AI-related research, and innovate new tools.
